# Writing Rules

**Teach, don't cite.** When referencing a paper, explain it well enough that the reader never needs to open the original to follow your argument. Write the version you wish existed when you first read it.

**Context before ideas.** Every new concept gets: what problem it responds to, what you were thinking when it appeared, then the idea itself, then what it means. Never drop something cold.

**Self-contained entries.** Any entry should be readable on its own. Tags let people go deeper. No entry requires homework.

**Personal voice.** This is you thinking, not performing. Wrong turns stay in. The humanity is the point.
